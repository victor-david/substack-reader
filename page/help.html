Substack Reader Extension

This extension applies to domains and sub domains of substack.com, and is used to override the theme set by the stack author.
When you click the extension icon, you can choose between the following:
  • Light - Stacks are displayed in a light theme.
  • Dark - Stacks are displayed in a dark theme.
  • Off - Stacks are displayed in the original theme set by the stack author.
A change applies to all sub domains of substack.com. When you make a change, if one or more tabs are opened to a subtack, those tabs are reloaded to apply the new theme.

Other Domains

When you click the extension icon while on a site that is not substack.com, you have the option of adding that site to the sites that are affected by the extension. This will only make a difference if the site is actually a substack, but is using a substack custom domain

Revoking Access

When you click the extension icon on a site that you've added, you have the option of revoking that access. You can also go the options for the extension and revoke all custom sites that you've added.
