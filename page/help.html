Substack Helper Extension

This extension applies to domains and sub domains of substack.com, and is used to override the theme set by the stack author.

When you click the extension icon, you can choose between the following:

  • Light - Stacks are displayed in a light theme.
  • Dark - Stacks are displayed in a dark theme.
  • Off - Stacks are displayed in the original theme set by the stack author.

A change applies to all sub domains of substack.com. When you make a change, if one or more tabs are opened to a subtack, those tabs are reloaded to apply the new theme.
